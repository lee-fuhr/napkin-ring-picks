How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests or time trials. Instead, we comb through published reviews from respected home and kitchen outlets, read hundreds of owner comments on retailer sites and forums, and look for patterns in long-term feedback. The napkins that win our picks are the ones people still love after six months or a year of regular washing and drying. We pay special attention to what actually breaks: frayed hems, stubborn stains, pilling, and shrinkage. A high rating here means the set comes out of the wash looking the same way it went in. We also note practical trade-offs, like linen’s need for ironing or polyester blends’ tendency to trap odours, so you know exactly what you’re getting into.
